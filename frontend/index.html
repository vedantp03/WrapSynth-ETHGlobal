Process

How It Works

Four simple steps to ferry your Monero to DeFi

1

Initiate Swap

User creates mint request with secret commitment and sends XMR to LP's Monero address

2

LP Confirms Receipt

LP verifies XMR receipt off-chain and marks mint request as READY on-chain

3

Reveal Secret

User reveals secret to claim wsXMR - verified on-chain using secp256k1 operations

4

Receive wsXMR

Smart contract mints wsXMR to user, backed by LP's collateral in vault
